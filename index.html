about

Kiran Arain is a multimedia artist and recent graduate of New York University. Their work spans filmmaking, performance, interactive media, and emerging technologies, exploring the intersections of storytelling, identity, memory, and collective imagination.

Drawing from screenwriting, installation, electronics, and digital tools such as Blender and TouchDesigner, they create projects that move fluidly between cinematic, theatrical, and experiential forms.
