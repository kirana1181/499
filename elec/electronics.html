TouchDesigner

Infection

Personalized projection-mapping simulating blood spreading in veins— overlayed with a video of memories and organs.... custom TouchDesigner pipeline.

Electronic/Animatronic

I Don't Blame You / Defunct Portal Turret

A reiteration of a robot featured in the game, Portal. Motion sensors trigger an intense light spasm when the structure detects a subject.

Automata & interactive projection

THE NAVIGATION THAT GUDES YOU

Servo-driven automata gives a spinning shadow to the wall— while interacters control a flowing beam of light that will follow their hands. Light can be programmed to follow, or run away from the hand.

Echo Chamber

Interactive Touchdesigner installation- different messages appear as the viewer slowly closes their eyes and or hides their face— symbolizing the messages we are too afraid to hear.
